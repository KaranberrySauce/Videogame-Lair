History

Developed by Microsoft, the once named "DirectX Box" was the first in the series of Xbox consoles. November of 2001, the Xbox made it's debut. Competing with the PS2 and many other sixth generation line of consoles it was able to keep up with competition Four years later, the Xbox 360 was released and it was competing with the PS3. This was part of the seventh generation line of consoles. This console sold over 78 million units in just 8 years. That same year of reaching that milestone the Xbox One was released which, yes, was and still is competing with Sony's PS4. A more lucrative and ambitious console was announced in 2016 called the Scorpio. Not much was said except that it would change the console history.
