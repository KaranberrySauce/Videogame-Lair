History
As time goes on, games get more and more advanced with new tech being created and used by the industry. It is important to know where they came from and how how they were created. As early as 1950s, what is believed to be the first videogame ever created was "Noughts and Crosses" or more formally known today as "Tic-Tac-Toe." 20 years later, the infamous "Pong" game was created and this was the turning point of the videogaming franchise, created by Atari. As games became more revolutionized, Entertainment Software Rating Board (ESRB) established an age restriction on most games. From 1986 onwards, games have changed dramatically and rapidly and the industry blew up very quickly making it one the fastest industry to reach billions in over a decade.
